Ingredients

  • 2.5 tablespoons yeast
  • 3.5 cups warm water
  • 1 cup soft oleo (almost)
  • 1 cup sugar (almost)
  • 2.5 eggs
  • 10 cups flour
  • 3 teaspons salt

Instructions

  1. Have yeast, oleo, sugar, and eggs ready
  2. Add water and mix with hands
  3. Add salt and enough flour to be able to knead dough
  4. Knead until smooth and elastic
  5. Cove and let rise in warm place, free from draft, until doubled in size
  6. Take 1/2 dough and roll into oblong shape
  7. Brush lightly with melthed margarine
  8. Sprinkle with mixture of sugar and cinnamon
  9. Roll up as for jellyroll
  10. Seal edges
  11. Cut into 1 inch pieces
  12. Place in greased pan, slightly touching
  13. Brush with melted butter
  14. Spinkle with a little sugar and cinnamon
  15. Set in warm place until double in size
