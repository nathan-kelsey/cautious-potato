Ingredients

Cake

  • 2 sticks oleo
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup light sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Icing

  • 3.5 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 stick oleo
  • 1.5 tablespoons cocoa
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Instructions

Cake

  1. Melt oleo
  2. Add cocoa, sugar, and water
  3. Mix well
  4. Add the remaining ingredients
  5. Mix well
  6. Pour into greased jellyroll pan
  7. Bake at 350o for 15 minutes

Icing

  1. Mix ingredients together with enough milk to make the right consistency
